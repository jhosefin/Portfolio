Social Network "Be my friend"

Be My Friend is an application designed to facilitate the adoption of animals looking for a home. Our platform gives you the opportunity to meet these adorable creatures and make a difference in their lives. With Be My Friend, you will be able to sign up and create a personalized account to start your adoption journey. Once you're logged in, you'll be able to create and edit posts about the animals you want to adopt, providing relevant information about their species, age, and personality. The application was developed as a Single Page Application with Firebase (Firestore, Authentication and Hosting) as backend service and vanilla JavaScript.
